Newbridge House & Farm Market

Newbridge House & Farm hosts the main farmers’ market serving Donabate. Set within the historic estate grounds, the market offers a mix of fresh produce, artisan foods, and family‑friendly stalls in a scenic parkland setting.

  • Fresh vegetables and seasonal Irish produce
  • Artisan breads, pastries, and baked goods
  • Local honey, jams, and small‑batch preserves
  • Craft cheeses, meats, and specialty foods
  • Street food and hot food vendors
  • Handmade crafts and local makers

Typically runs on weekends, with stall numbers varying by season.
