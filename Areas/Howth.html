Howth Market

Howth Market is the main farmers’ and artisan market in the area, located beside the harbour. It operates year‑round and is popular with both locals and visitors.

  • Fresh Irish vegetables and fruit
  • Local honey, jams, and preserves
  • Artisan breads, pastries, and baked goods
  • Craft cheeses and small‑batch producers
  • Hot food stalls and international street food
  • Handmade crafts and local gifts

Typically open on weekends and bank holidays.

Other Local Food Options

While Howth Market is the primary farmers’ market, the village also offers excellent food options:

  • Fresh seafood directly from Howth Harbour
  • Independent bakeries and cafés
  • Specialty food shops and delis

These complement the market and provide year‑round access to local produce.
