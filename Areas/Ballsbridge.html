Ballsbridge

Herbert Park Food & Craft Events (Inside Ballsbridge)

Herbert Park, located directly in Ballsbridge, hosts seasonal food and craft markets throughout the year. These events often include:

  • Fresh Irish produce and vegetables
  • Artisan breads and pastries
  • Local honey, jams, and small‑batch foods
  • Craft cheeses and specialty items
  • Street food and hot food stalls

Events are not weekly but occur during festivals, holidays, and special weekends.

Nearby Farmers’ Markets

For regular weekly markets, residents often visit nearby areas:

  • Grand Canal Dock Market — Fresh produce, baked goods, and street food.
  • Temple Bar Food Market — One of Dublin’s best Saturday markets with local producers.
  • Ranelagh Village Market — Artisan foods, vegetables, and baked goods.

All are easily accessible from Ballsbridge by bus, bike, or on foot.
