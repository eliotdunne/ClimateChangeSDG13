Airfield Estate Food Market

Airfield Estate is the main source of local, farm‑fresh food in Dundrum. While not a traditional weekly farmers’ market, it offers high‑quality produce directly from the estate and local Irish suppliers.

  • Seasonal vegetables grown on the estate
  • Fresh eggs and dairy from Airfield’s own farm
  • Artisan breads and baked goods
  • Irish cheeses and small‑batch products
  • Occasional food market events throughout the year

Opening hours Are from Friday and Saturday 11am - 2pm.
