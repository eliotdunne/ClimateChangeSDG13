About the Market

Honest2Goodness is one of Dublin’s most popular indoor food markets, located in Glasnevin. Known for its high‑quality produce, artisan foods, and friendly atmosphere, it attracts regulars from across the northside every Saturday.

  • Fresh fruit and vegetables
  • Artisan breads, pastries, and baked goods
  • Irish cheeses, meats, and charcuterie
  • Specialty foods, olives, oils, and spices
  • Street food and hot dishes
  • Local makers and small‑batch producers

Open every Saturday, with a strong mix of regular and rotating stalls.
